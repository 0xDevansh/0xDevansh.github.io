Frozen leaves hide life within \
Some embers go on smolderin' \
This quiet night has seen more light \
than what a day could dream to win

When time feels frozen, yet seems to flee \
I catch this moment in a cup of tea \
with a storm next door, a deafening roar \
it's the closest to home that I can be

Although it's true that you could do \
with a little more warmth handed out to you \
every silver lining needs a dark cloud to shine \
every golden hour preceded by a tinge of blue
